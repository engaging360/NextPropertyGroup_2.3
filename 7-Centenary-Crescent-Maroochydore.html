One of the largest blocks in the area, this substantial 994m2 property has abundant room for the children to play and plenty of space for a pool, if you so desire. This well-built brick home has recently undergone renovations which have only enhanced this stunning property. The original timber floor has been beautifully polished, showcasing the modern new kitchen which sits in the heart of this home. There are 3 good sized bedrooms plus the master with a fully renovated ensuite.

Walk out of either living area and you will find yourself on the verandah which hugs the back of this home. Here is the perfect backdrop to daily life as you sit and gaze over the rear garden which spills onto the leafy reserve of Nowingi Park.

This property is perfectly located for you to embrace the Sunshine Coast lifestyle with only a short stroll to the pristine patrolled beaches of either Maroochydore or Alexandra Headland, all the while conveniently positioned to appreciate an array of amenities including; the Sunshine Plaza, the new CBD development, Ocean Street and the surrounding dining precinct.

So, if you are searching for a peaceful family home where you can choose to leave the car at home and still enjoy all the sunshine coast has to offer...…then you’ve found it!
