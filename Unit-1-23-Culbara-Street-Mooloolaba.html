An enviable and magical lifestyle opportunity has presented itself in this tightly held area of the Sunshine Coast. Perfectly positioned with a north facing aspect and unrestricted deep water ocean access, this luxurious townhouse is situated in one of the most sought after Mooloolaba waterfront locations.

Architecturally designed with high ceilings, this immaculate residence combines the scale and feel of a home with the convenience of low-maintenance living. Sail the Pacific Ocean with direct deep-water access from your private pontoon, or stroll to the cosmopolitan Mooloolaba esplanade with its award winning restaurants and vibrant café culture.

An elegant union of custom-designed comfort, and immaculate attention to detail, this residence is spacious, practical and expertly tailored. A palate of neutral tones and tactile materials, including organic dark timber floors creates an ambiance of effortless sophistication. A stylish central glass atrium, planted with lush tropical greenery infuses the home with natural light. Offering an abundance of open plan living flowing over 2 levels and out to the breathtaking calm waters of the canal. The extensive use of glazing showcases the sparkling in-ground pool and expansive water views.

The perfectly appointed designer kitchen boasts ample integrated storage, including a walk in pantry with preparation space, premium appliances and immaculate finishes. Flowing seamlessly to the spacious Al Fresco undercover entertaining area with a perfect northerly aspect. Completing the appointments are 4 generous sized bedrooms plus an office with the choice of an ensuited master bedroom situated upstairs and downstairs for total convenience. Two separate living areas on both levels provide space and sanctuary when required.

A secure gated entry complete with garaging for two cars, additional storage and secure off-street parking for a boat provides ultimate privacy. This home has been designed to appeal to the discerning buyer who is considering “downsizing” but doesn’t want to compromise on location, quality or lifestyle.
